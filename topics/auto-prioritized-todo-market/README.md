## Question
Is there real, proven demand for a to-do product whose central promise is "given all
my tasks, tell me the best thing to do next"?

## Why it matters
Validates (or de-risks) the core bet behind the `noceremony` product before further
build investment.

## Key questions / sub-threads
- [x] Is there proven demand for automatic prioritization/planning?
- [x] How large and how fragmented is the baseline task-management market?
- [x] What validates automatic ordering specifically vs. generic to-do apps?

## Current state
Full market research report mined from the `noceremony` repo. Verdict: demand for
**automatic prioritization and planning** is proven (Motion, Reclaim, SkedPal,
Sunsama), but the market is segmented, and mass demand for this exact KPI formula is
not yet publicly proven. See findings.
